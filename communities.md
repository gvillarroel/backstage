## Communities

### AI Builders Guild

- **Type:** Guild
- **Cadence:** weekly demos
- **Description:** For engineers shipping copilots, workflows, and product-facing AI features.
- **Link:** [Join Guild](https://teams.microsoft.com/ai-builders)

### Model Risk Council

- **Type:** Council
- **Cadence:** biweekly review
- **Description:** For policy, legal, security, explainability, and review checkpoints before production rollout.
- **Link:** [Join Council](https://teams.microsoft.com/model-risk)

### Prompt Lab Office Hours

- **Type:** Office Hours
- **Cadence:** twice per week
- **Description:** For lightweight enablement on prompts, evals, agent instructions, and reuse patterns.
- **Link:** [Join Office Hours](https://teams.microsoft.com/prompt-lab)

### Data Science Forum

- **Type:** Forum
- **Cadence:** monthly deep dive
- **Description:** For experimentation, benchmarks, retrieval pipelines, and model quality measurement.
- **Link:** [Join Forum](https://teams.microsoft.com/data-science)
